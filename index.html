Have Questions? We've Got Answers!

Learn more about how Nchito360 helps your team manage leave, attendance, and HR tasks with ease.

What is Nchito360?

Nchito360 is a comprehensive HR management system that helps businesses streamline leave management, attendance tracking, and employee onboarding — all in one platform.

Who can use Nchito360?

Nchito360 is designed for Large Enterprise and SMEs, HR departments, and growing teams. Whether you're a startup or an established company, our system adapts to your workflow.

Can employees request leave online?

Yes! Employees can easily request or plan leave days directly from their dashboard. Managers can review and approve/reject with comments.

How does Nchito360 handle approvals?

Branch Managers and Company Admins receive notifications for new leave or join requests. They can approve or reject directly from the admin panel.

Is the system secure?

Yes. Nchito360 follows Laravel security best practices and uses role-based access to protect sensitive HR data.

How do I onboard my company?

Simply sign up, create your company, and start inviting your team. Employees can also request to join an existing company pending approval.
